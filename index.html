Skybox CriticalOps Executive Equity Sharing Program

Our initial year at Skybox CriticalOps was marked by a rapid to market entry and the successful launch of our hyperscale data center operations enterprise. To sustain this growth trajectory, competitive incentives directly tied to Skybox's value creation options have been requested by the executive team.

Opportunity: Aligning Incentives for Continued Value Creation

The core objective here is to motivate and retain our senior leadership and foundational talent. We'll achieve this by directly aligning their long-term interests with Skybox's ongoing success, fostering shared value among those crucial to our past and future growth, and carefully maintaining founder equity control.

The Proposed Solution: Strategic "Synthetic Equity"

We propose implementing a strategic 'synthetic equity' program. This program is designed to provide a financial upside directly linked to Skybox's performance, offering the benefits of equity participation without the complexities associated with actual share issuance.

Key Advantages of Phantom Stock Model

The Phantom Stock model offers significant benefits designed to help us retain talent, preserve founder equity, and drive continuous performance.

Zero Dilution

Founders maintain full equity control with this synthetic option.

Strong Alignment

This model incentivizes leadership to drive long-term company value and specific performance goals.

Cash Liquidity

It provides meaningful cash rewards, simplifying financial realization for recipients.

Effective Retention

Multi-year vesting schedules and performance periods encourage long-term commitment.

Consistent Cash Flow

Dividend equivalents offer consistent, periodic payouts, enhancing liquidity.

Flexibility

Each model can be tailored to our specific growth phases and leadership structure.
